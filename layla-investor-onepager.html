01 · THE PROBLEM
EGYPT GOES OUT.
NO ONE HELPS.
Cairo, Sahel, and Gouna host millions of nights out a year — yet the infrastructure serving those nights is fractured, unsafe, and analog.
🚨
Drunk driving is the norm
  • No affordable service drives drivers home with their car
  • Uber/Careem leave the car behind — a non-starter for Egyptians
  • Result: ~60% of nightlife-goers drive after drinking
📱
The scene lives in DMs
  • Events discovered through Instagram, WhatsApp, word-of-mouth
  • No trusted ticketing → fake tickets, overselling, scams common
  • House parties happen privately — no safe discovery layer for hosts or guests
02 · THE SOLUTION
FOUR PRODUCTS.
ONE APP. ONE LOGIN.
LAYLA is the first mobile super-app purpose-built for Egyptian nightlife. Four integrated products, unified by a single verified identity and social graph.
🎟️
EVENTS & TICKETS
Discover, book, QR-entry with anti-screenshot protection. Paymob, Fawry, Vodafone Cash, Apple Pay.
🏠
HOUSE PARTIES
Verified hosts. Hidden addresses until approval. First safe platform for private parties in the region.
🚗
SAFE RIDE VALET
A trained valet drives you AND your car home. Live tracking, SOS, insured. No one else does this at scale.
👥
COMMUNITY
Social feed, stories, DMs, venue follows, reviews. The connective tissue that makes the app sticky.

05 · WHY WE WIN
DEFENSIBILITY,
BY DESIGN.
🛡️ SAFETY AS A FEATURE, NOT A RETROFIT
ID-verified hosts, anti-screenshot tickets, valet background checks, SOS with voice trigger. Built in from day one — impossible to add later without rearchitecting.
🔗 FOUR PRODUCTS, ONE GRAPH
Every product feeds the others. Events discovered via friends. Valet ordered after check-in. House parties promoted to scene-mates. Network effects compound across pillars.
🚗 VALET IS UNFORGEABLE
Requires driver network, training, insurance, dispatch, vetting. Operationally heavy — a two-year moat against anyone trying to copy.
🇪🇬 LOCALIZATION DEEP-WINS
Arabic-first UX, Egyptian payment rails (Paymob/Fawry/VF Cash), cultural nuance in safety messaging, seasonal Sahel mode. International players will fumble this.
